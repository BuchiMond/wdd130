History

Founded in 2020 by a group of river enthusiasts, our company began as a small rafting outfit offering weekend trips. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted adventure brand, guiding thousands through powerful rapids. What started as a passion project has become a lifelong mission to connect people with rivers, adventure, and the raw beauty of nature.
