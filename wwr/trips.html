Trips

Ready for an adventure of a lifetime? Explore our thrilling trips and make unforgettable memories! Contact Us Today

Mountain Rapids Adventure

Take on the challenge of our most intense rafting trip! Navigate through rushing waters, steep drops, and thrilling twists. This trip includes all necessary safety equipment, a professional guide, and snacks for the journey.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Level: Advanced
  • Equipment: Helmet, life jacket, paddle

Family Fun Float

Perfect for all ages, this relaxing rafting trip allows you to enjoy the beauty of nature without intense rapids. Includes comfortable rafts, safety gear, and a friendly guide who shares fun facts about the river.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Level: Beginner
  • Equipment: Life jacket, paddle

Sunset River Cruise

Experience the breathtaking colours of the sunset from the water. This gentle rafting trip includes refreshments, soft lighting for evening paddling, and plenty of photo opportunities.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Level: Easy
  • Equipment: Life jacket, paddle

Jungle Expedition Rafting

Embark on a thrilling journey deep into the heart of the jungle. Paddle past lush greenery, spot exotic wildlife, and tackle exciting rapids. Includes lunch, safety gear, and an experienced guide who knows the secrets of the river.

  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Equipment: Helmet, life jacket, paddle
